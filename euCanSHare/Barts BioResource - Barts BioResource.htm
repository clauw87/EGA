The purpose of the Barts BioResource is to establish a repository of generic consented patients clinically managed by Cardiac Services at Barts Health NHS Trust. In addition to this, the consent may include the donation of human blood, saliva, urine, and tissue donation (e.g. arteries, veins and heart tissue) for the purpose of establishing a biobank of medical data and samples for use in cardiac disease research.
